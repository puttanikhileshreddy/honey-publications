Processing Fee

Honey open access publication provides easy access of all its publications to all the readers and knowledge seekers. Anyone can easily read or download the articles without any payment. In order to publish the high quality journals, each article undergoes many steps like editing, proofreading, plagiarism check, quality check, web maintenance and so on. Our team works on a banal process to give the quality output.

In order to justify the work that involved before every article is published, we charge Article Processing Fee (APF). We cover the cost of web hosting, database, CMS, archiving, peer review, online repository, internet, electricity and employees’ salaries. The only funds we receive for all these are the publication fee as we don’t get any funds from anywhere else. So, the publication fee will be paid by the authors and we give the conformation once we receive the payment.
