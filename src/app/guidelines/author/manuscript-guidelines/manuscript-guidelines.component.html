Manuscript Guidelines

We welcome manuscripts that are either submitted online or mailed to the concerned editor mail id. The accepted manuscripts become the Honey Publications undeniable property. Along with manuscript, we request our authors to send a cover letter with your details and co-author’s details.

Types of Manuscripts :
  • Editorial
  • Research
  • Review
  • Case Report
  • Others
Manuscript Organization
Running Head
  • Characters: Up to 50
  • Font: Calibri
  • Size: 14
  • Alignment: Left, Running head should be added in the header along with the page numbers.
  • Type of Article:: Research/ Case Report/ Review/ Opinion/ Short Communication/ Mini Review/ Editorial.
Title
  • Title Words: Up to 20
  • Font: Calibri
  • Size: 16
  • Alignment: Centre; Title should be in bold and in initial caps.
Authors

List all the author names like Author1, Author2 and Author3

  1. Author Department, University, Country
  2. Author Department, University, Country
  3. Author Department, University, Country
Corresponding Author

Author name, Affiliation, Address, City, State, Country, Tel: ...; Fax: …; E-mail:
Title
  • Words: Up to 20
  • Font: Calibri
  • Size: 16
  • Alignment:Centre (Title should be in Bold and in initial caps).
Headings
  1. Abstract
    • Words: Up to 300
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • Abstract should incorporate a brief content of the article.
  2. Keywords
    • Words: Up to 10
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • The major keywords used in the article should be mentioned here.
  3. Abbreviations
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • If there are any abbreviations in the article, they should be mentioned here.
  4. Introduction
    • The goal of introduction is to convey basic information to the readers. Well return introduction set the tone of the paper and catch the reader’s interest.
  5. Discussion
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • Discussion must depict and portray the significan/ce of the findings.
  6. Conclusion
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • An effective conclusion should provide termination for a paper, leaving the reader feeling satisfied that the concepts have been fully explained.
  7. Acknowledgement
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • Provide the list of individuals who contributed in the work and grant details.
  8. Conflict of Interest
    • Font: Calibri
    • Size: 12
    • Declare if any financial interest or any conflict of interest exists.
    • Note*: If there are any sub headings in the body text, sub-categorize them accordingly under the heading in which they fall.
    • For Example:
      • 1. Heading
      • 1.1 Sub Heading
      • 1.1.1 Sub-sub-heading
References
  • Font: Calibri
  • Size: 12
  • All references should be cited in the article in a consecutive order. List here all the references in numbered order of citation in the text. List all authors if less than six. If more than five authors, list the first five followed by “et al.”
  • Note*:Provide the link for the listed references.
Figures
  • Figures should be clear with high resolution.
  • Figure Legends: Description of figures/images.
  • Size: 12
  • Font: Calibri
Tables
  • Font: Calibri
  • Size: 12
  • Alignment:Centre
  • Table:Brief descriptive title of the table
  • Table Abbreviations: Give here full form of all abbreviations used in the table. Give the full form even if it has bees explained in the text.
