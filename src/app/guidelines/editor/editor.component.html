Editors & Associate Editors

Editors and Associate Editor plays a key role in reviewing and editing the manuscripts. They are responsible for quality, consistency and impact of our journals. Honey Publicationâ€™s editors stick to the Committee of Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines in order to address the ethical issues that may arise.

  • Editors perform the review process which includes the quality parameters of the journal
  • Any content published in our journals should be approved by our editors
  • Any article/book will be approved by the editors only if it reaches the quality, relevance, validity and clarity.
  • Editors should ensure that the requirements of readers and authors are met through constant endeavours.
  • The editors are requested to review the content in privacy as the submitted material should be kept confidential.
  • Editors should be available to publish the content on corrections, clarifications, retractions and apologies if needed.
  • Editors should ensure that all the published articles are properly and permanently archived in database.
  • Editors must guarantee that the publishers dwell by the laws of intellectual property rights and not violate them by any means
  • Editors support the researchers in educating them about good practises in publication ethics.
