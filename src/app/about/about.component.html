Our Moto

Honey is an open access Publication established like a hive of articles and books. As honey bees collect honey from different flowers and make a hive, our Honey Publications team collect articles from many authors and publish them all at one place. Our main motto is to create a medium between authors and knowledge seekers for sharing the knowledge in the form of articles and books. We believe that wealth shared with others may decrease but, knowledge shared will never. Knowledge sharing enlightens many brains. Honey Publications is a mixture of different journals. Knowledge seekers/readers can have the free access of quality edited articles and books published online by the approval of our scholarly editors. We welcome all the authors to share their knowledge. Besides scientific and technical journals, we also publish stories and novels in three different languages. English Hindi Telugu Our team try hard to publish the journal content as soon as possible.
