Journals

All the journals in Honey Publications are open access. Our articles/books undergo peer review before they are published. Honey Publications keep updating our journals to improve the content and to satisfy our readers. Our journals include Engineering, science, medicine, technology, fashion and so on. We publish any fictional stories or books and novels in three languages. Our journal’s quality will be assured by our reputed editors and peer review process. Any manuscript/ book submitted will be published in the relevant language so; authors are requested to submit the manuscripts/books in the respective language.

Journal of Engineering JOE

Honey Publications welcome the students, professors, scholars with any engineering background to submit your articles or books. Any submission regarding Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronic, Civil engineering will be accepted in our journal. JOE aims for high quality and quick publications. The motto of our journal is to provide the future generations in engineering background to gain vast knowledge in their desired field. This journal accepts various manuscripts like research, review, mini review, case reports, short communications, opinions, perceptions and so on.
